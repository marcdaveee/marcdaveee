Reflecting this Journey

My internship experience here at Pixel8 really solidified my growth not only as a developer, but as a better professional in the future. This company has provided me with the necessary challenges I needed in order to step up my skills, especially in front-end development. But those challenges didn't only help me to improve my development skills, but also taught me "grit". Being introduced to how they structure their development projects was a bit overwhelming for me at first. But I didn't let it discourage me; instead, I made huge adjustments by allotting most of my time to figuring out their project structure. That's why I'm really thankful to myself because I didn't give up and took it as an opportunity to grow, which really paid off in the end.

As an intern, I really enjoyed the process of learning their tools. I was able to utilize a front-end framework (VUE JS), which helped me to develop modern web UIs more effectively. Moreover, I was also exposed a lot to collaborative development with the use of Git and GitLab tools, which was really cool because I started to get comfortable with it! Another memorable experience in this journey was when I was assigned to become a team leader of a team. As someone who wasn't exposed to leadership roles, this opportunity to become a team leader really brought a lot of growth to me. It taught me to communicate effectively with my members, manage and organize tasks well, and most especially, improved my leadership skills.

I'm indeed grateful for this journey with Pixel8!
